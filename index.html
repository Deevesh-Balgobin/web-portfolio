About Us

Welcome to ArtDeal.mu, your number one source to purchase your art works. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of art works, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2019 by SDK , ArtDeal.mu has come a long way from its beginnings in a studio. When SDK first started out, their passion for providing the best artwork for their fellow clients drove them to do intense research and gave them the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Mauritius and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the artistic industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Deevesh Balgobin, Founder
