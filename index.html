About Us

Welcome to custom furniture, your number one source to construct any wood furniture design you want. We are dedicated in giving you the very best of our work service, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. When Deevesh Balgobin first started out, his passion for providing the best furniture for his fellow clients drove him to do intense research and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online website. We are now serve customers all over Mauritius and receive good feedback.

We hope you enjoy our service as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
